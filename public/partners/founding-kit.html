Who reads Toxome

Toxome's readers are, overwhelmingly, women who came to check what is actually in their clothes. They read the label before they buy. That is the audience a partnership puts your brand in front of.

Their top concerns

They are health-first, not trend-first.

Toxome's readers do not arrive for a look. They arrive worried about what they cannot see on the label, and they buy accordingly. This is what they are actually avoiding.

  • Endocrine disruption

    Hormone-mimicking chemicals in synthetic dyes, finishes, and coatings, and what they do over sixteen hours against skin.

  • Toxic chemical load

    PFAS, formaldehyde, azo dyes, and the "forever" finishes sprayed onto conventional clothing.

  • Microplastics

    Plastic fibers shedding off synthetic fabric onto the body and into the home, all day.

  • Hormone health

    PCOS, endometriosis, and fertility, the conditions that send many of them looking in the first place.

  • Skin reactivity

    Irritation and sensitivity from chemically treated fabric worn directly against skin.

Our rules, in the open

A brand can never buy a better score.

These hold for every partner. We put them in the offer, not the fine print, because the one that reads as a liability is the one that makes the rest worth anything.

  1. The Toxome Rating is never for sale. Money buys amplification of a score already earned. It never buys points, a preview, or an appeal.
  2. Brands are scored and published before they are ever contacted about a partnership.
  3. Every paid placement is visibly labeled Featured Partner, with the rating rendered exactly as it is beside it.
  4. If a partner's score drops below the top band, the placement is pulled immediately, with no refund. Compositions change, so ratings change.
  5. A Journal feature is guaranteed to run, and always labeled. The verdict inside it is never for sale: no copy approval, house voice, rating as it is. You buy the placement, not the opinion.
  6. Paid placements never occupy more than half the featured surface.
  7. Partnership is not a ranking. No partner may be presented, by Toxome or by itself, as best, number one, or top-rated.
